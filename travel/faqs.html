How do I sign up?

Go to our online form where you can enter and complete the registration form

What is the minimum age required to join a tour?

You must be 18 years and above to join.

Does the package include insurace coverage?

Yes! Travel insurance, health insurance, and life insurance come bundled with the package. Insurance is provided by TD Insurance.

What activities are included in the adventure package?

The adventure package includes activities such as skydiving, mountain climbing, sailing, and a canopy walk.

Are meals provided during the trips?

Yes, most of our packages include meals during the trips. Specific details will be provided based on the selected package.

What is the cancellation policy for the packages?

You can cancel your package up to 48 hours before the start date for a full refund. After that, partial refunds may apply.

Do I need any special equipment for the activities?

All necessary equipment for the activities is provided by our partners. However, you are welcome to bring your own if preferred.
