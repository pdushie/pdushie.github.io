Project 3

Air Quality Index Predictor

Air Quality Index (AQI) is a measure of the level of polution in the air. It is an important measure since it provides information on the quality of air we breath in and its impact on our health. The AQI predictor app uses Decision Tree Regression a machine learning approach to predict the AQI given the mean concentration of Nitrogen dioxide (NO2), Sulphur dioxide (SO2), Ozone (O3), and Carbon mono oxide (CO). The AQI predictor was trained on the United States air pollution dataset from the year 2000 to 2016. You can see a demo of the app by clicking view more. everything when you can.
